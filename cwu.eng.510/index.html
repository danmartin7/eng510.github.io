A History of Composition Theory and Teaching Writing

This webtext is a community outreach and service-learning project. CWU students taking ENG 510: Composition Theory conduct and share research about the history of composition and teaching writing. Students also write stories, create critical timelines, and complete other relevant projects on the history of teaching writing and composition theory. The purpose of this webtext is to engage the questions and ideas the community has about the history of teaching writing. The goal of this webtext is to provide the most accurate research and information about teaching writing and composition theory. The projects on this webtext were designed and created in ENG 510: Composition Theory at Central Washington University. This graduate level course provides students an opportunity to study and research the history of teaching writing and the socio-political and cultural history associated with that history.
